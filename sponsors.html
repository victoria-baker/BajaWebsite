Elite Sponsors

Cornell University is a vital part of Baja Racing at Cornell. They provide a facility full of resources to enable members and allow the team to truly turn their ideas for the car into reality.

SolidWorks empowers Baja Racing at Cornell with industry-leading 3D CAD software, enabling team members to design, visualize, and simulate complex components with precision, transforming innovative ideas into reality.

ANSYS works on engineering simulation by helping the world's most innovative companies deliver radically better products to their customers. They offer software to help solve complex design and engineering challenges.

The Gleason Works is a competence center for the development of state-of-the-art manufacturing equipment for all types of bevel gears and is also one of Gleason’s global footprint plants, producing bevel and cylindrical gear machines as well as tooling products.

G&C Design and Fabrication specializes in precision manufacturing, providing advanced machining services to produce complex components with exceptional accuracy and efficiency.

Siemens drives innovation in industrial automation and digitalization, offering advanced software, hardware, and services to enable smarter manufacturing and efficient engineering solutions worldwide.

Gold Sponsors

KISSsoft, a Gleason company, develops advanced calculation software for designing, optimizing, and validating machine components. Their flagship products, KISSsoft® and KISSsys®, simplify our drivetrain modeling and seamlessly integrates with CAD software.

TeamGantt is an intuitive project management software that helps teams and individuals plan, schedule, and track projects. They provide tools for effective collaboration and seamless workflow management.

3Dconnexion provides advanced input devices designed for 3D modeling and CAD professionals, enabling precise navigation and efficient workflows for even the most demanding design applications.

Hygear Suspension provides premium suspension solutions, offering custom tuning and performance upgrades to optimize handling and control for a variety of vehicles and applications.

GMN supplies manufactured precision bearings, sprag clutches, and non-contact seals to OEMs, MROs, and distributors in North America while providing world-class engineering support and technical services.

Michigan Scientific Corporation (MSC) provides engineering services specializing in the design and manufacturing of slip ring assemblies, transducers, signal conditioning electronics, and data acquisition.
