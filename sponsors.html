Gold Sponsors

The Gleason Works is a competence center for the development of state-of-the-art manufacturing equipment for all types of bevel gears and is also one of Gleason’s global footprint plants, producing bevel and cylindrical gear machines as well as tooling products.

KISSsoft - a Gleason company - is the creator of innovative calculation software used in a wide variety of industries to design, optimize, and verify machine elements. KISSsoft AG's flagship products KISSsoft® and KISSsys® can be used to easily model and analyze a drivetrain in a package that is well integrated with CAD software.

GMN supplies manufactured precision bearings, sprag clutches, and non-contact seals to OEMs, MROs, and distributors in North America while providing world-class engineering support and technical services.

Michigan Scientific Corporation (MSC) provides engineering services specializing in the design and manufacturing of slip ring assemblies, transducers, signal conditioning electronics, and data acquisition.
